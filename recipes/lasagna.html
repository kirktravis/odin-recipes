Lasagna

Brittan'ys wonderful yummy delicious Skillet Lasagna

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes! You can use ground turkey in place of beef, if you like.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 (28 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1.2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil (Optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oreganon (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups dried malfada noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
